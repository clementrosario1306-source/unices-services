Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?
Go to My Bookings, find your booking and click the Cancel button. Cancellations are free up to 2 hours before the scheduled time.
How do I reschedule?
Click the Reschedule button on your booking card and pick a new date and time slot.
When will I get a refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.
Are the professionals verified?
Yes! All Unices professionals go through background checks, skill tests, and identity verification before joining our platform.
